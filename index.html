About Krampus

Krampus is based on the terrifying Christmas demon from Alpine folklore. In tradition, while Saint Nicholas rewards good children with gifts, Krampus punishes those who have misbehaved. This game brings this dark legend to life in an exciting and atmospheric adventure.

Key aspects of Krampus include:

  • Immersive horror gameplay set in a Christmas theme
  • Atmospheric environments inspired by Alpine villages
  • Unique blend of stealth and action mechanics
  • Rich storytelling based on authentic folklore

The Legend of Krampus

Krampus originates from Alpine folklore, particularly in regions of Austria, Bavaria, Croatia, and other Central European areas. This fearsome creature is said to be the counterpart to Saint Nicholas, punishing children who have misbehaved throughout the year.

Traditional characteristics of Krampus include:

  • Half-goat, half-demon appearance
  • Carries chains and bells
  • Appears on Krampusnacht (December 5th)
  • Known for taking naughty children away in his sack
